HELPING ANIMALS SAVING LIVES


LIFECARE Charitable Trust is a non-revenue driven association established with an aim to limit human-animal struggle and give relief to animals in trouble. We run two groups - one for untamed life and the other for domestic/street animals and have given guide straightforwardly to over 68,000+ animals since beginning.

about us

together we can save the planet

LIFECARE Foundation is established by visionaries who who felt the need to build a free of cost medical facility for the stray animals where the best, most advanced, ethical and highly skilled medical care is readily available.We truly believe that animals are the real victims on earth.

Our mission is to end the human/street dog conflict by creating a humane and sustainable environment for animals with direct benefits to the people of India.Our vision is a world where humans and animals coexist in sustainable ecosystems. Our mission is to build awareness and implement effective systems such that habitats and animals are protected.we not only save animals from suffering, we also rehabilitate and release them back into the wild and work to protect their precious natural habitats.

Street Animal Rescue

We treat animals in critical condition at our clinic and recovery center. Car accidents, wire traps, illnesses and skin diseases like mange are some of the issues we see.

Humane Education

We visit schools and villages to educate them about rabies and compassion towards animals. Our goal is to develop a community partner in caring for street animals.
